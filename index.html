General Conduct

Always respect others in the server. Disrespectful behaviours include offensive language, personal attacks, posting inappropriate images or gifs, and any form of racism.

Race Night and Console Type

We race crossplay. Drivers must ensure stable connectivity. Repeated poor connection may result in being moved to the reserve list.

  • Sunday Nights: Full qualifying and 50% races.
  • Wednesday Nights: Short qualifying + 35% and 25% races. The 25% race grid is the reverse of the 35% race results.

Attendance

Attendance must be marked at least one hour before the race. If a seat becomes available, it will be given to a reserve driver.

Full-time drivers missing two consecutive weeks may lose their seat and be moved to the reserve list.

Qualifying

Drivers must not use intermediate or wet tires unless conditions require it. Blocking another driver on a flying lap is prohibited.

Five-Minute Break

A 5-minute break follows qualifying or the sprint session. Drivers may use this time to report incidents, take a quick break, or prepare for the race.

Racing

Race starts are hectic; approach with caution to avoid early incidents. Always give space to other drivers, and avoid dive-bombing or intentional crashing.

Drivers rejoining the track must do so safely. Resetting to track is prohibited and will result in penalties.

Reserve Drivers

Reserve drivers must mark attendance using the reserve icon. They can qualify but do not score points in the championship.

Safety Car

Drivers must maintain a safe distance during safety car periods and avoid overtaking unless indicated. Lapped cars must be allowed to pass the safety car when prompted.

Incident Reporting

Drivers have until 7 PM the day after the race to submit incident reports. Submissions must include the reported driver, description, session details, and video evidence.

Failure to provide POV footage when requested may result in penalties, including warnings or bans.

FIA Appeals

An incident can be appealed to the FIA if you feel the stewards have made the wrong decision. Provide your original POV and any new evidence with your appeal.

Stewards Penalties

The penalties listed serve as a guide for stewards. Penalty points are applied based on infractions, and repeated offenses can lead to severe consequences.

License Point System

Drivers accumulate penalty points for infractions. Points thresholds result in penalties, such as race bans or disqualifications. Points drop after clean races.
