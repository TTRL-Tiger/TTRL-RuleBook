General Conduct

Always respect others in the server. Disrespectful behaviors include offensive language, personal attacks, inappropriate content, and racism.

Race Night and Console Type

We race crossplay. Drivers must ensure stable connectivity. Repeated poor connections may result in being moved to the reserve list.

Attendance

Attendance must be marked at least one hour before the race. If a seat becomes available, it will be given to a reserve driver.
