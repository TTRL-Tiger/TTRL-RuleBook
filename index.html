Introduction General Conduct Race Night Attendance Qualifying Penalty System

Introduction

Welcome to the Torque Titans Racing League (TTRL). This rulebook provides the guidelines for participating in our league. Please read the rules carefully to ensure fair and competitive racing.

By participating in TTRL, you agree to abide by these rules and uphold the values of respect, sportsmanship, and clean racing.

General Conduct

Always respect others in the server. Disrespectful behaviors include offensive language, personal attacks, inappropriate content, and racism.

Race Night and Console Type

We race crossplay. Drivers must ensure stable connectivity. Repeated poor connections may result in being moved to the reserve list.

Attendance

Attendance must be marked at least one hour before the race. If a seat becomes available, it will be given to a reserve driver.

Qualifying

Drivers must not use intermediate or wet tires unless conditions require it. Blocking another driver on a flying lap is prohibited.

Penalty System

The following table outlines the penalties for various infractions:

Offense Details Penalty
Minor Blocking Impeding during a hot lap 1 Penalty Point
Major Blocking Damage and impeding during a hot lap 2 Penalty Points
Severe Blocking DNF caused as a result of blocking 5-second Time Penalty (next race)
Unsportsmanlike Behavior Offensive behavior or server rule violations Up to League Ban
